Major Challenges & Ethical Dilemmas

Deepfakes & Misinformation

  • Fake videos of public figures
  • Spread of false information
  • Identity theft and fraud
  • Political manipulation

Real Deepfake Cases

How to Spot Deepfakes

  • Blinking Patterns: Unnatural or no blinking
  • Audio Sync: Voice doesn't match lip movement
  • Facial Edges: Blurry boundaries around face
  • Background: Distorted or inconsistent

Job Displacement

  • Voice actors and musicians
  • Video editors and animators

  • Need for new skill adaptation

Activity 1: AI Video Creation

Using MiniMax.io


Steps:

  • Visit MiniMax Hailuo Video
  • Use Google Gemini to create video prompts
  • Generate a 5-second video clip
  • Experiment with different styles

Video Prompt Examples

Simple: "A dog running in a park on a sunny day"


Detailed: "A golden retriever running through green grass in a park, cinematic shot, warm lighting, slow motion, professional quality"


Creative: "An astronaut floating in space with Earth in the background, peaceful and serene, 4K quality"

Using Gemini for Better Prompts

Ask Gemini:

"Help me write a prompt for AI video generation. I want a video about [your idea]. Make it detailed and cinematic."


Include: Subject, setting, mood, camera style, lighting

Activity 3: AI Music Generation

Using Suno.com


Steps:

  • Create account on Suno
  • Use Gemini to write music prompts
  • Generate a short song
  • Try different genres and moods

Music Prompt Examples

Simple: "Happy pop song about friendship"


Detailed: "Upbeat acoustic folk song about summer adventures, with guitar and harmonica, cheerful vocals, 3 minutes long"


Specific: "Lo-fi hip hop instrumental for studying, peaceful and calming, with soft piano and subtle beats"

Ask Gemini for Music Ideas

Prompt for Gemini:

"Create a detailed music prompt for Suno AI. I want a [genre] song about [topic] that sounds [mood]. Include specific instruments and style details."


Try different genres: Pop, Rock, Jazz, Electronic, Classical, Country

Prompt Engineering with Gemini

Tips for better AI-generated content:

  • Be specific and descriptive
  • Include style, mood, and genre
  • Mention camera angles for videos
  • Specify duration and pace
  • Use Gemini to refine your prompts

Prompt Structure Template

Content type: [Video/Audio/Music]

Subject: [Main subject/character]

Setting: [Location, time, environment]

Action: [What is happening]

Style: [Visual style, genre, artistic influences]

Mood: [Emotional tone, atmosphere]

Technical: [Camera angle, lighting, quality]

Using Gemini to Improve

Gemini Prompt Template:

"I'm using [tool name] to create [content type]. My initial prompt is: [your basic idea]. Please help me improve this prompt with more details about style, mood, technical specifications, and creative elements to make the output more professional and aligned with my vision."
